Why Choose us?

Welcome to Warung Badik 2, where happiness meets extraordinary flavors. Our culinary haven offers a delightful fusion of local delicacies and tempting Western classics. From mouth-watering nasi goreng to appealing chicken chop, each dish is a masterpiece of passion and taste. Open from rice time till night, we ensure everyone can indulge in our delightful offerings. Our warm hospitality and eco-conscious practices complement the inviting ambiance, making your dining experience truly memorable. Join us on a gastronomic journey filled with love, artistry, and the joy of shared moments. At Warung Badik 2, every bite brings a taste of happiness and a dash of culinary magic.
